About Chiropractic

Chiropractic in North Vancouver


Chiropractic is a healing art, grounded in science. Having been practised all over the world for over one hundred years, Chiropractic has grown into one of the most popular alternative healing methods. It focuses on the links between the spine and central nervous system with overall health by the correction of dysfunctional segments in the spine. These dysfunctional segments are called "subluxations", meaning "less than a dislocation"


Dr. Pearce started Seymour Chiropractic after graduating from the Southern California University of Health Sciences in Los Angeles, as a Doctor of Chiropractic (DC), in 2002.
