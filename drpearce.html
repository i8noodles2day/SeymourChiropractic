Dr. Pearce

Dr. Pearce enjoys sharing the benefits of Chiropractic with other outdoor enthusiasts

Dr. Pearce started Seymour Chiropractic after graduating from the Southern California University of Health Sciences in Los Angeles, as a Doctor of Chiropractic (DC), in 2002.

In determing a practice location, Dr. Pearce chose Deep Cove in North Vancouver. The proximity of North Vancouver to the mountains is advantageous, as Dr. Pearce would like to share the benefits of Chiropractic with other outdoor enthusiasts on the North Shore. Dr. Pearce treats many Vancouver area athletes such as mountain bikers, skiers, snow-boarders, and others who choose to pursue active lifestyles.
